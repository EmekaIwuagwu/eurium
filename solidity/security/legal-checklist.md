# Legal & Compliance Checklist for Eurium (EUI)

This document outlines key regulatory and legal considerations for the issuance of a Euro-pegged stablecoin. This is not legal advice.

## 1. Regulatory Classification (EU/MiCA)
- [ ] Determine if Eurium falls under **Asset-Referenced Token (ART)** or **Electronic Money Token (EMT)** categories under MiCA.
- [ ] Obtain required VASP or EMI licenses from relevant national regulators (e.g., BaFin, ACPR).
- [ ] draft and publish the required **White Paper** as per MiCA Title IV.

## 2. AML/KYC Compliance
- [ ] Integrate with a reputable KYC provider (e.g., Chainalysis, Elliptic).
- [ ] Implement on-chain gating (e.g., checking for KYC/AML flags before finalizing redemptions).
- [ ] establish Sanctions monitoring (OFAC/EU lists).

## 3. Reserve Management
- [ ] Draft legal agreements with custodial banks for Euro deposits.
- [ ] Define legal ownership of reserves (Direct vs. Trust model).
- [ ] Schedule monthly independent audits and daily proof-of-resorts.

## 4. Terms of Service
- [ ] Define redemption rights (legal claim on the issuer).
- [ ] Specify fees (minting, redemption, maintenance).
- [ ] Clause on "Extreme Market Conditions" (De-pegging procedures).

## 5. Intellectual Property
- [ ] File trademark for "Eurium" and "EUI" in target jurisdictions (EU, US).
- [ ] secure relevant domains (eurium.finance, etc.).
